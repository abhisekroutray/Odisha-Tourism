(PHUNTSOKLING) A trip to the Land of Happiness and Plenty The name "Phuntsokling" means "Land of Flourishing Happiness" in the Tibetan language, and the settlement truly lives up to its name. The community is known for its vibrant culture, which is a blend of Tibetan and Indian influences. Visitors to Phuntsokling can experience traditional Tibetan music and dance performances, as well as sample authentic Tibetan cuisine.

why choose?

Tibetian Heritage Awaits You

One of the main attractions in Phuntsokling is the Namgyal Institute of Tibetology, which is dedicated to the study and preservation of Tibetan culture and history. The institute has a vast collection of books, manuscripts, and artifacts related to Tibetan Buddhism, as well as a museum that showcases Tibetan art and culture. Overall, Phuntsokling is a wonderful destination for those interested in Tibetan culture and history, as well as for those seeking a peaceful and serene getaway amidst the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayan mountains.
