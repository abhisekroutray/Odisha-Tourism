Paradise Awaits You

Koraput is mostly famous for its Jagannath Temple which is also known as Sabara Srikhetra. Srikhetra is normally referred to as Puri Jagannath, but the unique identity of the Koraput temple is because no section of society is barred entry. The traditional culture (including languages), knowledge and subsistence of the Adivasis are closely connected with local ecosystems.
